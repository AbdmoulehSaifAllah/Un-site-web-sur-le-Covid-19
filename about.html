What you need to do

How To Protect Yourself

Use warm water and soap and rub your hands for at least 20 seconds. Work the lather to your wrists, between your fingers, and under your fingernails. You can also use an antibacterial and antiviral soap.

You should do

  • Stay at home
  • Wear mask
  • Use Sanitizer
  • Disinfect your home
  • Wash your hands
  • Frequent self-isolation

You should avoid

  • Avoid infected people
  • Avoid animals
  • Avoid handshaking
  • Aviod infected surfaces
  • Don't touch your face
  • Avoid droplets
